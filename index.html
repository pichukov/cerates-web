Currency Exchange Rates App

About

The Currency Exchange Rates app is a reliable and hassle-free solution for staying updated on currency exchange rates. With a user-friendly interface and a wide range of features, it provides accurate and up-to-date exchange rates for over 150 currencies.

Features

  • Real-time exchange rates sourced from trusted financial institutions
  • Currency converter with easy-to-use interface
  • Filters and search function for convenient currency selection
  • Supports dark mode for comfortable viewing in low-light conditions

Privacy Policy

The Currency Exchange Rates app takes your privacy seriously. As a free application, we do not collect any personal information from our users. We do not track your usage or share your data with any third parties.

Please note that while the app itself does not collect personal information, it may require permissions to access certain device features, such as internet connectivity, in order to provide the currency exchange rates. These permissions are necessary for the proper functioning of the app and do not involve the collection or sharing of personal data.
